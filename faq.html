How It Works

The Model, Explained

What exactly is pay per lead?⌄

Pay per lead means you only pay for leads that are actually delivered to you — not for time, not for ad spend, not for retainers. At LeadEvolution, you agree on your criteria upfront (vertical, location, volume), and you pay per lead once it's delivered to your CRM. Simple, transparent, risk-free.

How do you source your leads?⌄

We source prospects from targeted data lists across Australia — business databases, industry registries, and proprietary datasets. Every prospect on our list is then approached individually and converted with their explicit consent. They agree to be contacted by a specialist in your field before we ever pass the lead to you. This is what sets our leads apart from recycled or scraped data.

How do you qualify leads before sending them to me?⌄

Every lead goes through a qualification process that confirms: (1) they match your geographic and demographic criteria, (2) they have a genuine need for the product or service, (3) they have expressed explicit interest in speaking with a specialist, and (4) they are not on the Do Not Call Register (for consumer leads). You only receive leads that have passed all four checks.

How quickly will I receive my first leads after signing up?⌄

Typically 24 hours from onboarding. Once your criteria are confirmed — vertical, location, volume, any specific requirements — we begin sourcing immediately. There's no lengthy ramp-up period. Most clients receive their first leads within one business day.

How are leads delivered to me?⌄

Leads are delivered directly into your CRM the moment they're qualified. We support API and webhook integration with GoHighLevel, Salesforce, HubSpot, and most major CRM platforms. If you don't use a CRM, we can deliver via email or a simple spreadsheet — whatever fits your workflow.

Are your leads exclusive?⌄

Yes. All LeadEvolution leads are 100% exclusive. When a lead is delivered to you, it goes to you alone — not shared with competitors, not recycled through multiple buyers. This is a non-negotiable part of our model. Shared leads drive down close rates and erode trust; we don't operate that way.

Pricing & Contracts

Transparent Pricing

How much do leads cost?⌄

Pricing is based on a few factors including your vertical, target criteria, and volume requirements. There are no setup fees, no monthly retainers, and no hidden charges — you simply pay per lead delivered. Book a free audit or contact us to get a quote tailored to your specific needs.

Is there a minimum order or lock-in contract?⌄

No contracts, no lock-ins, no minimum commitments. You buy leads when you need them and pause whenever you want. Most clients start with a batch of 10–20 leads to validate quality for their specific vertical, then scale up as confidence builds. We earn your ongoing business by delivering — not by locking you in.

Are there any setup fees?⌄

None. Zero setup cost, zero onboarding fee. You pay per lead delivered, and that's the only charge you'll ever see from us.

Can I negotiate volume pricing?⌄

Volume arrangements are available for clients committing to 100+ leads per month consistently. Please contact us or book a call to discuss your specific volume requirements and we'll put together a tailored proposal.

What payment methods do you accept?⌄

We accept bank transfer (EFT), credit card, and direct debit. Payment terms are discussed during onboarding based on your preferred volume and frequency. We work with clients to find a payment structure that suits their cash flow.

Lead Quality & Guarantee

Our Quality Standards

What happens if a lead doesn't meet the agreed criteria?⌄

We replace it. LeadEvolution operates a Lead Replacement Guarantee: if a delivered lead doesn't match your agreed criteria — wrong geography, wrong intent, not qualified, or simply not interested — we replace it at zero cost within 48 hours. No arguments, no process, no credit notes. A replacement goes into your CRM.

What if a lead doesn't convert into a sale?⌄

Lead conversion is ultimately a function of your sales process — and that's your domain, not ours. What we guarantee is that the lead meets the agreed qualification criteria. If it does, the lead stands. We can't guarantee close rates, but we can guarantee a quality pipeline. That said, our founder Jordan has spent a decade in sales across financial services — if you want a second opinion on your conversion process, he's happy to discuss it.

How do I report a lead for replacement?⌄

Simply email info@leadevolution.com.au with the lead details and the reason for the replacement request. We review all replacement requests within 24 hours and process confirmed replacements within 48 hours. Straightforward.

Can I set specific criteria for my leads?⌄

Yes. During onboarding we work with you to define your ideal lead profile: geographic targeting (state, postcode range, metro vs regional), demographic requirements, specific product or service interest, and any exclusion criteria. The more specific your criteria, the better we can match supply to your exact pipeline needs.

Compliance & Legal

Compliance You Can Count On

Are your leads DNCR compliant?⌄

Yes. LeadEvolution holds a Do Not Call Register (DNCR) Type B licence. All consumer phone numbers are washed against the DNCR before delivery. B2B outreach to licensed professionals (mortgage brokers, financial advisers, business owners) operates under the standard B2B exemption — these calls are not subject to DNCR restrictions. We take compliance seriously because your licence depends on it as much as ours does.

How do you handle the Privacy Act 1988?⌄

All data collected, stored, and transferred by LeadEvolution is handled in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth). Every lead is obtained with the prospect's informed consent. Our data practices are documented in our Privacy Policy, and we conduct regular compliance reviews to ensure we meet all obligations under Australian privacy law.

Are your leads consent-based?⌄

Yes — this is one of our core differentiators. Every prospect is sourced from targeted data lists and individually approached. Before a lead is passed to you, the prospect has explicitly consented to be contacted by a specialist in their area of interest. This is not passive opt-in data — it is active, informed consent, which dramatically improves both lead quality and the experience for the prospect.

Is LeadEvolution ASIC registered?⌄

LeadEvolution is a registered business name under Artificial Innovations Pty Ltd (ABN 53 677 049 946), which is ASIC registered. We operate as a lead generation business and do not hold an Australian Financial Services Licence (AFSL). The leads we generate are passed to appropriately licensed advisers and brokers who conduct all regulated financial services activities.
