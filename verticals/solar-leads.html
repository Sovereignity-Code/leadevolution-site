Frequently Asked Questions

Can I target battery storage add-on leads specifically?⌄

Yes. We can qualify leads for battery storage retrofits separately from new solar system installations. During onboarding, specify whether you want new system leads, battery add-on leads, or both.

Do you cover commercial solar installations?⌄

We primarily generate residential solar leads, but can source SME and commercial rooftop solar leads for installers who have the capability and accreditation for commercial jobs.

What's the typical system size of your solar leads?⌄

Our residential solar leads typically represent 6.6kW–13.2kW system sizes, which are the most common in the Australian residential market. System size can be filtered during onboarding based on your installation capacity.
