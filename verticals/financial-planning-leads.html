Exclusive Financial Planning Leads for Australian Advisers

Australia's $3.5 trillion superannuation industry and the ongoing transfer of wealth between generations create one of the world's most fertile markets for financial advice. But reaching high-quality prospects — people with real assets, real questions, and real readiness to engage — is where most adviser practices struggle.

LeadEvolution sources financial planning leads from targeted data sets covering Australians who are at key financial decision points: approaching retirement, receiving an inheritance, selling a business, or seeking to optimise their super. Each prospect is individually approached and converted with explicit consent before the lead reaches your CRM.

What Each Financial Planning Lead Includes

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Primary area of interest (super, SMSF, retirement, wealth management, insurance)
  • Estimated investable assets or super balance range
  • Employment status (employed, self-employed, retired, near retirement)
  • Geographic location and state
  • Confirmed consent to speak with a financial adviser

The Financial Advice Market Opportunity

With over 2 million Australians holding self-managed super funds and the average SMSF balance exceeding $750,000, the opportunity for qualified advisers is extraordinary. The challenge is that traditional lead generation for financial services is slow, expensive, and heavily compliance-constrained.

LeadEvolution solves this by building compliance into the lead generation process from day one. Every lead is consent-based, DNCR-washed, and delivered in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988. You get warm, qualified prospects without the compliance risk of cold outreach.

Financial Planning Lead FAQs

Can I target SMSF leads specifically?⌄

Yes. SMSF leads are one of our most-requested financial planning sub-categories. We target existing SMSF trustees looking for advice, as well as prospects considering setting up an SMSF for the first time.

Do you provide leads for superannuation advice?⌄

Yes. Our founder Jordan spent years in superannuation sales and understands this market deeply. We can target Australians approaching retirement, those receiving employer super contributions they want to optimise, and those seeking advice on consolidating multiple super accounts.

Can I specify minimum asset thresholds?⌄

Yes. We can target leads by estimated asset range or super balance, ensuring leads match your minimum client profile. This is particularly important for advisers operating under a minimum FUM or assets-under-advice model.
