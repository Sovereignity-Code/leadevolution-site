The Australian SME Funding Market

Australia has over 2.5 million small and medium businesses, and access to commercial finance remains one of the most common growth constraints reported by business owners. The commercial lending market processes hundreds of billions annually, with specialist brokers increasingly taking market share from direct-to-bank channels. The challenge is finding business owners at the right moment — when a funding need is active, a decision-maker is engaged, and timing is right to present a solution.

Why Commercial Finance Leads Are Hard to Source — And How We Solve It

Commercial finance leads are notoriously difficult to generate at scale because the qualification bar is genuinely high. A lead that doesn't have an active ABN, verifiable turnover, and a real funding need within a defined timeframe is worthless to a commercial broker. LeadEvolution's qualification process verifies all three criteria before a lead is ever delivered — meaning every conversation you have is with a real business owner who has a real need and the means to proceed.

The Economics of Commercial Finance Lead Generation

The average commercial finance deal generates $8,000–$25,000 in broker commission for a $250K–$1M deal. With our exclusive, pre-qualified leads, closing just 2 deals at average commission ($16,000) from a batch of 20 leads represents an exceptional return on lead spend. For brokers specialising in larger commercial transactions, the economics are even more compelling.

Frequently Asked Questions

What loan sizes do your commercial leads typically represent?⌄

Our commercial finance leads are pre-qualified for funding needs in the $100K–$5M range. During onboarding you can specify a minimum deal size to ensure leads match your lender panel and commercial preferences.

Do you supply equipment finance leads specifically?⌄

Yes. Equipment and asset finance leads are a distinct category we can qualify separately. These leads are pre-screened for business type, asset type (vehicles, machinery, technology, fitout), and purchase timeline.

Can I target specific industries for commercial finance leads?⌄

Yes. During onboarding we can configure industry filters — for example, hospitality, healthcare, construction, retail, or transport — based on your lender panel strengths and preferred deal types.
