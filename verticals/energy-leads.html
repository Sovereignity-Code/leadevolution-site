Australia's Deregulated Energy Market

The deregulation of Australia's electricity and gas markets in QLD, NSW, VIC, and SA has created one of the most dynamic retail energy switching environments in the world. Millions of households and businesses are paying above-market rates on default tariffs, and the switching industry has grown to billions in annual commission revenue. The key challenge for brokers and retailers is identifying prospects who are both eligible to switch and actively motivated to do so.

Why LeadEvolution Energy Leads Are Different

Most energy lead providers supply lists of households who have vaguely expressed interest in saving money. Our energy leads are pre-qualified switching prospects: individuals or business decision-makers who have confirmed their current contract status, expressed active intent to switch, and consented to speak with an energy specialist. The difference in conversion rate between a warm, qualified switching lead and a cold interest-expressed list is dramatic.

The SME Energy Opportunity

Small and medium businesses typically spend $15,000–$80,000 annually on energy costs and are significantly more likely to switch providers when presented with a compelling comparison. SME energy leads at LeadEvolution are pre-qualified for business type, annual energy spend range, and switching timeline — giving brokers the highest-probability commercial conversations in the market.

What We Qualify in Every Lead

  • Current contract end date confirmed
  • Active switching intent verified
  • Recent bill available for comparison
  • NMI/MIRN captured where possible
  • State and current retailer identified

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I target business energy leads only?⌄

Yes. During onboarding you can specify residential-only, SME-only, or both. SME energy leads carry additional qualification criteria including business type, annual energy spend, and decision-maker confirmation.

What states do you cover for energy leads?⌄

We generate energy switching leads in deregulated markets: QLD, NSW, VIC, and SA. The ACT market is available in limited volume. We do not currently generate WA energy leads as that market remains regulated under Synergy.

What is the average annual bill size of your energy leads?⌄

Residential leads typically represent households with $2,000–$5,000 annual energy spend. SME leads represent businesses spending $15,000–$80,000 annually. Both segments can be filtered during onboarding based on your commission structure.
