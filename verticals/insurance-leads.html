The Australian Insurance Protection Gap

Research consistently shows that the majority of Australians are significantly underinsured — with the average household facing a life insurance shortfall of over $600,000. Income protection coverage is even more concerning, with millions of working Australians having no protection against illness or injury. This protection gap represents an enormous pipeline opportunity for advisers who can reach the right prospects at the right time.

Why LeadEvolution Insurance Leads Convert

Our insurance leads are generated through targeted outreach to working Australians who have acknowledged a gap in their current coverage. You're speaking to someone who has already accepted they need advice — your job is to close, not to educate from scratch.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I target health insurance leads?⌄

Our current insurance lead focus is life insurance, income protection, and TPD — the advised insurance categories. Health insurance lead generation requires a different compliance framework and is not part of our current offering.

Do you supply group or business insurance leads?⌄

Yes. We can generate key person insurance and business succession planning leads for advisers who specialise in the business insurance market. These require additional qualification criteria around business size and structure — discuss during onboarding.

What is your qualification process for insurance leads?⌄

Each prospect is individually outreached and qualifies by confirming: employment status, that they currently have inadequate or no coverage, intent to review within 14 days, and explicit consent to be contacted by an insurance specialist. Only leads meeting all criteria are delivered.
