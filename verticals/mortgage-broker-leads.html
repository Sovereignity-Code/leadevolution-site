Mortgage Broker Lead FAQs

Can I target specific loan types only?⌄

Yes. During onboarding you can specify loan type preferences — for example, refinance-only leads, first home buyer leads, or investment property leads. We tailor our qualification criteria to match.

Our mortgage leads span the full range of residential loan sizes. During onboarding, you can specify a minimum loan amount to ensure leads match your commercial preferences.

Yes. SMSF property leads require additional qualification given the complexity of borrowing requirements. We can source SMSF-specific leads for brokers who are accredited to write SMSF loans.
