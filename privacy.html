Artificial Innovations Pty Ltd, trading as LeadEvolution ("we", "us", "our"), is committed to protecting the privacy of personal information we collect, handle, and store. This Privacy Policy explains how we comply with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles ("APPs").

By using our website, submitting an enquiry, or engaging our services, you consent to the collection and handling of your personal information in accordance with this Policy.

1.What Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Website enquiry data — full name, email address, phone number, business name, business details and any information you voluntarily include in a message or form submission.
  • Booking data — appointment details submitted through Calendly or similar scheduling tools.
  • Lead recipient data — for clients who engage our services, we handle data about individual prospects ("Leads") generated through our outbound campaigns, including name, phone number, email, and qualification information captured during consented voice conversations.
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device type, and pages visited on our website (via standard server logs and minimal analytics).

2.How We Collect It

  • Through forms submitted on leadevolution.com.au;
  • Through Calendly bookings for consultations or kickoff calls;
  • Through inbound emails, phone calls, and direct correspondence;
  • Through consent flows embedded in Callable AI voice campaigns (for Lead recipient data).

We collect personal information directly from the individual wherever reasonably practical, and only to the extent necessary for the purposes described in this Policy.

3.Why We Collect It

We collect personal information to:

  • Provide our lead generation services to clients;
  • Respond to enquiries and fulfil requests made through our website;
  • Communicate with prospective and existing clients about services, campaigns, and account management;
  • Issue invoices and manage billing;
  • Conduct voice-based qualification conversations with Leads, with their consent;
  • Meet our legal, regulatory, and compliance obligations.

4.How We Store It

Personal information is stored on secure servers located in Australia. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption. Access is restricted to authorised personnel who require the data to perform their role.

Consent records relating to Leads generated through Callable AI campaigns are retained for a minimum of two (2) years from the date of consent, in line with our compliance obligations. Other personal information is retained only for as long as it is needed for the purposes described in this Policy, or as required by law.

5.Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for third-party advertising purposes.

We may disclose personal information to:

  • Callable AI platform — the voice platform operated by Artificial Innovations Pty Ltd, used to deliver outbound campaigns on behalf of our clients;
  • Our clients — where you have consented during a qualification call, your details are delivered exclusively to the single client running the campaign;
  • Service providers — trusted third-party providers (for example, Xero for invoicing, email service providers, cloud hosting) who are bound by confidentiality and data-handling obligations;
  • Regulators and law enforcement — where disclosure is required by law.

6.DNCR Compliance

We scrub every outbound calling list against the Australian Do Not Call Register before any campaign is run, in accordance with the Do Not Call Register Act 2006 (Cth). Numbers registered on the DNCR are excluded from all campaigns.

7.Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct any information that is inaccurate, incomplete, or out of date;
  • Delete your personal information, subject to any lawful retention obligations;
  • Withdraw consent to marketing or campaign communications at any time;
  • Lodge a complaint if you believe we have handled your information in a way that breaches the APPs.

To exercise any of these rights, email info@leadevolution.com.au. We will respond within a reasonable time, and in any event within thirty (30) days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

8.Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality and basic analytics only. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, behavioural tracking pixels, or cross-site trackers. You can disable cookies via your browser settings at any time without impact to the core functionality of the site.
