Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026  ·  Last updated: 27 April 2026  ·  Version 1.1

This Privacy Policy explains how Artificial Innovations Pty Ltd (ABN 53 677 049 946), trading as LeadEvolution ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, discloses, and manages personal information in connection with our lead generation services and website at leadevolution.com.au.

We are committed to protecting personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

1. What Information We Collect

We collect personal information from two groups of people:

Prospects (individuals whose information we generate as leads):

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • State/territory and general location
  • Industry-specific qualification data (e.g. loan purpose, investable assets, energy usage, insurance status)
  • Consent records including date, time, and method of consent

Clients and website visitors (businesses that purchase leads):

  • Business name, ABN, and contact details
  • Name and email of authorised representatives
  • Service preferences, volume requirements, and CRM delivery details
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party payment providers)
  • Website usage data via cookies and analytics tools

2. How We Collect Information

Prospect data is collected through direct outreach and qualification calls, during which each individual is clearly informed of the purpose of data collection and provides explicit consent before their information is passed to a client.

Client and visitor data is collected through our website contact forms, direct communications, and onboarding processes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate, qualify, and deliver leads to our clients
  • To manage client accounts and deliver our services
  • To communicate with clients about their account, orders, and service updates
  • To comply with our legal obligations, including DNCR compliance
  • To improve our website and services through analytics
  • To respond to enquiries submitted via our contact form

4. Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

Important: In the course of delivering our services, personal information may be disclosed to third parties, including parties located overseas. By using our services or submitting your information, you acknowledge and consent to these disclosures.

We may disclose personal information to:

  • Our clients — the businesses that purchase leads from us, for the purpose of following up on the consent-based enquiry
  • Technology and communications platforms — including Callable AI and similar voice automation and outreach tools used in our lead qualification process. These services may process data on servers located outside Australia (including in the United States and other jurisdictions)
  • Offshore team members and contractors — who assist with lead qualification, data processing, and operational support. These individuals are subject to confidentiality obligations and are required to handle data in accordance with Australian privacy standards
  • CRM and data delivery platforms — used to securely transmit lead data to clients
  • Payment processors — who handle client billing securely under their own privacy frameworks
  • Analytics providers — including web analytics tools that collect anonymised usage data
  • Regulatory bodies — where required by law, including the ACMA and the Do Not Call Register

Where personal information is disclosed to overseas recipients, we take reasonable steps to ensure those recipients handle data consistently with the Australian Privacy Principles. However, we may not always be able to guarantee equivalent privacy protection in all overseas jurisdictions.

5. Do Not Call Register (DNCR) Compliance

We operate in compliance with the Do Not Call Register Act 2006 (Cth). All prospect data sourced for B2C purposes is washed against the DNCR prior to any outreach. Consent-based leads are collected with explicit permission for a specific category of contact, consistent with DNCR exemption requirements.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, and unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. Lead data is transmitted to clients via secure, encrypted channels. Access to personal information is restricted to authorised personnel and contractors on a need-to-know basis.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by law. Lead data that has been delivered to clients is not retained by us beyond the delivery period unless required for compliance or dispute resolution purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Withdraw consent for future contact (opt-out)
  • Lodge a complaint about how we have handled your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@leadevolution.com.au. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Cookies and Website Analytics

Our website uses cookies and analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our site. This data is collected in aggregate and anonymised form and is not linked to individual identities. You can disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some website features may not function correctly as a result.

10. Complaints

If you believe we have breached your privacy, please contact us in the first instance at info@leadevolution.com.au. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at www.oaic.gov.au.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at leadevolution.com.au/privacy.html. Continued use of our services following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
