The Founder Who Became His Own Best Client

Jordan Gomez has spent his career at the sharp end of sales — not theorising about it, but doing it. Across more than a decade in financial services, Jordan has sold superannuation products, self-managed super fund (SMSF) solutions, property investment & development, and automotive and consulting services. He knows what it takes to close a high-value financial services client, and more importantly, he knows what it feels like to be handed a lead that wastes everyone's time.

That frustration — experienced repeatedly, across multiple employers and verticals — planted the seed for LeadEvolution. Jordan watched colleagues burn hours chasing unverified, recycled, or simply irrelevant leads purchased from agencies that prioritised volume over quality. He saw talented salespeople lose confidence not because they couldn't sell, but because the top of their funnel was broken.

"I kept thinking: if I could just speak to someone who actually wants what I'm offering, I'd close them. The problem was never the product. It was never my ability. It was always the leads." That realisation led Jordan to build the agency he wished his employers had used — one built with a salesperson's mindset, not a marketer's.

A Career Built in the Trenches

Jordan's sales career spans some of Australia's most complex and high-trust financial services categories. He has worked in superannuation advising everyday Australians on retirement planning, in SMSF where the sophistication of the buyer demands exceptional credibility, in property investment & development where deal structures require both financial acumen and relationship depth, and in automotive and consulting services where the sales cycle can span months and the stakes are significant.

This breadth of experience gave Jordan an unusually complete picture of what a qualified lead actually looks like — across different verticals, different buyer psychologies, and different sales processes. He doesn't guess at what makes a good mortgage lead versus a good financial planning lead. He's worked both sides of those conversations.

Why LeadEvolution Exists

LeadEvolution was built on one principle: give salespeople the leads they actually deserve. Exclusive leads — not recycled across five competitors. Consent-based leads — prospects who have actively agreed to speak with a specialist. Qualified leads — people with a genuine need, the intent to act, and the means to do so.

Jordan built LeadEvolution as the antidote to every bad lead experience he and his colleagues endured. The pay-per-lead model was a deliberate choice: if the lead isn't good enough, you shouldn't pay for it. Simple. It's the model that puts LeadEvolution's skin in the game alongside every client — and it's what ensures the quality standards never slip.

"I've sat in sales seats where I'd have killed for a service like this. I know exactly what a sales professional needs from their lead gen partner. That's not something you can learn from a marketing textbook — you learn it by doing the job."
