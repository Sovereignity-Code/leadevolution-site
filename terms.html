2.Services

LeadEvolution provides AI-powered outbound lead generation Services. Our Services include:

  • Configuration of a Campaign tailored to the Client's ideal customer profile, vertical, and geography;

  • Delivery of Leads on an exclusive basis, meaning each Lead is delivered to one Client only within their agreed vertical and geography;
  • Weekly reporting on volume, qualification outcomes and Campaign performance.

3.Lead Quality & Replacement Policy

LeadEvolution warrants that each Lead delivered will meet the qualification standards agreed in the Lead Supply Agreement. Where a Lead does not meet those standards due to genuine bad data, LeadEvolution will replace the Lead at no additional cost within twenty-four (24) hours of a valid claim being raised.

Valid replacement claims include:

  • Duplicate contact number (same lead previously delivered in the preceding 90 days);
  • Disconnected or non-functioning contact number;
  • Contact works in an industry outside the agreed vertical;
  • Contact details materially inaccurate or fabricated.

Claims that do NOT qualify for replacement:

  • The Lead went cold after delivery due to the Client's delayed follow-up;
  • The Lead changed their mind after qualification;
  • The Client failed to contact the Lead within a reasonable time;
  • The Lead did not convert, close, or purchase;
  • The Client is dissatisfied with conversion rates achieved on delivered Leads.

Replacement claims must be raised in writing to your Account Manager within seven (7) days of the Delivery Date. Claims raised outside this window are deemed accepted.
